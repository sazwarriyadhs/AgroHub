## 1. Executive Summary

**AgroHub** is an integrated digital agritech platform designed to accelerate the transformation of Indonesia's agricultural sector through modern technology based on:

| Technology | Description |
|:---|:---|
| 🤖 **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** | Plant disease diagnosis & recommendations |
| 🛒 **Agriculture Marketplace** | Agricultural product trading hub |
| 💳 **Financial Technology (Fintech)** | Digital wallet & escrow payment |
| 🌾 **Smart Farming** | Modern agricultural technology |
| 📦 **Supply Chain Management** | Supply chain management |
| 📚 **Education & Community** | Farmer education and community |

> 🌱 **AGRICULTURE SUPER APP PLATFORM**
>
> Connecting farmers, vendors, buyers, distributors, communities, and financial services in one modern digital ecosystem.

---

## 2. Background

Indonesia's agricultural sector has enormous economic potential, but still faces fundamental challenges:

### Main Problems

| Stakeholder | Problems |
|:---|:---|
| 🌾 **Farmers** | Difficult market access, dependence on intermediaries, lack of digital education, no access to modern technology, lack of real-time price information |
| 🛒 **Buyers & Distributors** | Difficulty finding trusted suppliers, low product quality transparency, inefficient supply chain |
| 🏪 **Agricultural Vendors** | Difficulty reaching broad markets, still manual sales operations |
| 📊 **Agricultural Ecosystem** | Long distribution chains, lack of technology integration, low agricultural sector digitalization |

---

## 6. Project Scope

AgroHub development includes several main systems:

### 📱 A. AgroHub Mobile App

Focused on field operational activities and mobile users.

| Role | Key Features |
|:---|:---|
| 👨‍🌾 **Farmer** | Farmer dashboard, Doctor AI, Market prices, Harvest management, Wallet, Education |
| 🏪 **Vendor** | Product management, Orders management, Seller monitoring |
| 🛍 **Buyer** | Mobile marketplace, Order tracking, Wallet |

### 🌐 B. AgroHub Web App

Focused on large marketplace and management systems.

| Role | Function |
|:---|:---|
| 🛒 **Public Marketplace** | Public marketplace for buyers and guest users |
| 🏪 **Seller Center** | Vendor/seller operational panel |
| 🖥 **Superadmin Panel** | Full platform management & monitoring |
