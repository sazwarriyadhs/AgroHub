>

---

## 2. Background

Indonesia's agricultural sector has enormous economic potential, but still faces fundamental challenges:

### Main Problems

| Stakeholder | Problems |
|:---|:---|
| 🌾 **Farmers** | Difficult market access, dependence on intermediaries, lack of digital education, no access to modern technology, lack of real-time price information |
| 🛒 **Buyers & Distributors** | Difficulty finding trusted suppliers, low product quality transparency, inefficient supply chain |
| 🏪 **Agricultural Vendors** | Difficulty reaching broad markets, still manual sales operations |
| 📊 **Agricultural Ecosystem** | Long distribution chains, lack of technology integration, low agricultural sector digitalization |

## 9. System Architecture

AgroHub adopts a scalable multi-platform ecosystem architecture designed to support millions of users across Indonesia's agriculture sector.

```text
Mobile Apps (Flutter)
│
├── AgroHub Farmer
├── AgroHub Vendor
├── AgroHub Customer
├── AgroHub Aqua
├── AgroHub Herd
└── AgroHub Express

Web Applications (Next.js)
│
├── Public Marketplace
├── Seller Center
├── Superadmin Platform
└── Corporate Dashboard

Backend Services
│
├── Authentication Service
├── Marketplace Service
├── Payment Service
├── AI Agriculture Service
├── Logistics Service
├── Notification Service
└── Analytics Service

Data Layer
│
├── PostgreSQL
├── Redis Cache
├── Object Storage
└── AI Model Storage
```

### Core Architecture Principles

* Modular and scalable microservice-ready architecture
* Multi-tenant platform design
* API-first development approach
* Cloud-native deployment strategy
* High availability and disaster recovery support
* Secure financial transaction processing
